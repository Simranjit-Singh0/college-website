SQUARE 1



Square 1 is every students' get to place in every free lecture! With so many options to choose what to eat, one is sure to get confused... It consists of 3 floors and hence can accomodate a lot of students at a time. First floor houses many outlets like the Havmor, La Pinoz, Chai Nagri and Burgrill . It also has the Tea Q, which gives you the feel of sitting in a cafe in and sip coffee with brownies! The 2nd foor lets you to the flavours of South India with The Banana Leaf. Sq1 works on a top-up basis where every student has to recharge their cards in order to buy anything from here. This can be a little time consuming standing in long queues to top-up...




SQUARE 2





Designed by the combined efforts of teachers and students of architecture, Square 2 has a great outlook and ambience. It is much smaller in size compared to Sqaure 1, however has enough seating to accomodate around 100 students. It has reasonable rates and quality maintained food. Pao bhaji is a particular favourite of every student in sq2. This cafeteria works with cash and hence is much less of a hassle.
