The University provides many facilities and amenities that the students can avail for convinience and joy.

LIBRARY





From traditional books and serials to films, and multimedia and networked information from around the world. Our libraries are a learning space where students are inspired to explore, research and create.
EXPLORETORIUM



Exploretorium is the inhouse auditorium of the University where important functions are organized and students get a chance of showcasing their talents.
EXPLORE HUB



Explore Hub, an incubator cum accelerator provides an apt environment for Student Startups to think, deliberate and work on new business ideas, innovate and evolve...
DISPENSARY



A healthy body makes for a healthy mind. In order to ensure your well-being, Chitkara University has tie- ups with renowned hospitals to take care of any kind of emergency.
CHITKARA SPECTACLES



The Museum showcases the Chitkara Glory since its inception and gives you a glimpse of its Academic Heritage.
GO GLOBAL



Go Global is the office of International Affairs which gives students the liberty and access of international studies and various exchange programs.
